Thesis

Creators are not just marketing. They are small media companies with a product and a distribution edge. When the talent is real, capital and tooling can compound fast.

We look for creators who treat the work like a craft and a game. Show up, iterate and keep your build clean.

We want to be the quiet upgrade behind the stream. Better tooling, better decisions and more runway.

How we invest

  • Systems We build repeatable workflows so you can ship like a team

We are selective. We do not want a hundred loose bets. We want a few strong builds.
